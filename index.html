WHAT ARE MOMOS?
Why We Chose That Name "Yummy Momo"! 
Momos (pronounced mo-mos) are meat or vegetable filled dumplings originally from Nepal and Tibet. Ours are completely hand made starting with local flour based casings and are filled with chicken, Panner or veggie mix. We serve them with Yam’s tomato, coriander and garlic sauce.
